About Me

Hi there! I’m Dr. Navya Nanduri ✨ A passionate dentist on a mission to craft healthy, confident smiles. I earned my BDS from Gitam University (2020) and completed my MDS at Sibar Dental Institute (2024), I was also honored with gold medals in Orthodontics and Microbiology during my BDS. With expertise in endodontics and restorative dentistry, I combine precision, dedication, and a patient-first approach to deliver exceptional dental care. Whether it’s perfecting smiles or restoring dental health, I ensure every treatment reflects my strong clinical foundation and meticulous attention to detail.

I believe every smile has a story, and I’m here to help yours shine its brightest!
